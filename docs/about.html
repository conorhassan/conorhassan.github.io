About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the ELLIS Institute Finland and the Probabilistic ML group at Aalto University.

Experience

I have held a variety of roles across research and industry:

  • Amazon — Applied Scientist intern, building personalized recommendation systems
  • WearOptimo — Data Scientist at a biotech startup developing microwearable devices
  • IMC — Quantitative trading intern
  • EY — Consulting intern
  • AgResearch — Statistical Scientist intern

I have also worked as a research assistant and tutored seven courses in mathematics and statistics.

Education

Prior to that, I completed two degrees at the University of Otago: a BSc in Statistics (minor in Mathematics) and a BSc Honours in Statistics. Highlights include:

  • Otago Institute Prize for the top Honours student across the Faculty of Science
  • Gopi Jain Memorial Prize for the top Statistics Honours student
  • Prime Minister's Scholarship for Asia for an exchange programme to NUS
